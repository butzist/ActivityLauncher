Support the continued development of Activity Launcher by upgrading to Activity Launcher Pro—an ad-free, analytics-free tool that unlocks hidden app activities, lets you create custom shortcuts, and helps power future improvements.

Whether you're a power user, developer, or just curious, Activity Launcher Pro gives you deeper control and customization over your device.

🔍 Key Features:
- Launch hidden or internal activities within installed apps
- Create shortcuts for quick access to specific features or screens
- Clean and lightweight with a simple interface
