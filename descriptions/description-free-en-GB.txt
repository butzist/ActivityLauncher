Easily launch hidden app activities and create custom shortcuts!

Discover the full potential of your Android apps with Activity Launcher – a powerful tool that lets you access hidden activities and create home screen shortcuts for any installed app.

Whether you're a power user, developer, or just curious, Activity Launcher gives you deeper control and customization over your device.

🔍 Key Features:
- Launch hidden or internal activities within installed apps
- Create shortcuts for quick access to specific features or screens
- Clean and lightweight with a simple interface

💡 About the Project:
Activity Launcher is based on the open-source project available at https://github.com/butzist/ActivityLauncher.
